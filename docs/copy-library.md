## Objections

### “Why pay for a beta?”

The ₹299 is for the complete seven-day guided experience, not early access to an unfinished feature list. The beta is small so I can support access issues directly and learn what is genuinely useful before expanding it.

### “Is this therapy?”

No. MindFlow is a private journaling and self-reflection tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional care.

### “Can I use ChatGPT?”

You can use a general AI tool for prompts. MindFlow keeps one bounded process together: the prompt, private entry, reflection, and progress, so you do not have to design the experience yourself.

### “What happens to what I write?”

Your entries are tied to your account and are not shared with other users. The founder does not read entries for routine support. Entry text is sent to OpenAI only when you consent to receive AI reflections. You can permanently delete your account and journal data.

### “What happens after beta?”

You will have 30 days to move your data into the full app. If you do not migrate, your beta account and journal content will be deleted after that window.

### “What if I miss a day?”

Previously unlocked days stay available. You can continue without losing entries; beta completion is measured across ten days.
